Our Mission & Vision.

Vision

To empower the Communities living in a just peaceful, democratic society united in diversity and enjoying quality of life and God given dignity

Mission

Committed to reducing human suffering and poverty by empowering vulnerable, marginalized and displaced communities in Tanzania to achieve self-reliance and sustainable development
